What is POSitiveFlow?

POSitiveFlow is the result of a collaborative project by five enthusiastic students from the web development course at KFUPM. We set out to create an innovative POS solution that simplifies transactions and enhances business operations. Our teamwork and dedication reflect our passion for technology and commitment to delivering a great user experience.
